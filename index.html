About Our Club

The UGB English Club is a student-led organization dedicated to improving English language skills through engaging activities, cultural exchange, and academic support.

Our Mission

To create a supportive environment where students can improve their English proficiency through interactive and enjoyable activities while fostering cross-cultural understanding.

We believe that language learning should be fun, engaging, and relevant to real-life situations.

Conversation Practice

Regular discussion sessions on various topics to help members gain confidence in speaking English.

Cultural Exchange

Learn about different cultures while practicing English through themed events and international guest speakers.

Academic Support

Workshops and tutoring sessions to help with English coursework, presentations, and exam preparation.

Community Building

Connect with like-minded students who share your passion for language learning and cultural exchange.

Why Join Our Club?

  • Improve your English skills in a friendly, non-judgmental environment

  • Meet students from different majors and cultural backgrounds

  • Gain confidence in public speaking and academic writing

  • Access exclusive learning resources and workshops

  • Participate in fun cultural events and language games
